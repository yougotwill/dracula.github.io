Vivaldi

Using theme

  1. Go to Vivaldi Menu > Preferences > Themes
  2. At the bottom of the window, look for a plus sign
  3. Enter following colors
  4. Background: #282a36
  5. Foreground: #f8f8f2
  6. Highlight: #6272a4
  7. Accent: #44475a
